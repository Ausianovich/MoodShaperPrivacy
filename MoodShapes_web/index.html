What's inside

A tiny ritual that adds up.

Tap, tag, look back. The whole flow is built around the smallest possible moment of attention — and the longest possible memory of it.

Log a mood in one tap

Pick a shape, add a few tags, and you're done. No essays, no scoring, no streaks shaming you when life happens.

See the curve of your week

Mood curves, counts, and a beautiful donut roll up your check-ins so the patterns show themselves — without a spreadsheet.

A month at a glance

The calendar heatmap turns thirty small taps into one honest picture of the month. Some weeks are green. Some aren't. Both are okay.

Your moods stay on your phone.

No accounts. No real names. Mood entries, notes, photos, tags and self-test answers live locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch — never on a server we run.
